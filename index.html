Welcome to the 2017 Dallas Badminton Singles Championships!

We are pleased to invite you to participate to the fourth Dallas Badminton Singles Championships, at Churchill Recreation Center and powered by Ruchi Palace, on Saturday March 25th, 2017. The Singles Championships is a club-independent effort to promote the practice of singles in the Dallas Fort Worth area.

We provide participants with excellent playing conditions, including a gym with high ceiling, very good shuttles (Yonex AS-30), and matches played in best of three 21 point games.

All participants get lunch and a take-home souvenir.

We are very grateful to our host, Churchill Rec. Center, and to our generous sponsors Ruchi Palace, the Sophia Design Group and XPrudent, without whom we could not have created such event at the affordable overall cost of $30 per participant.

Events

Men Singles, Senior Men Singles, Women Singles, and Senior Women Singles

Events with less than 3 players will be cancelled.
Players may only participate in one event.

Number of matches and scoring

Each player is guaranteed 2 matches. All matches are best of three games using the Badminton World Federation 21-point rally scoring system.

Draws

DRAWS AND SCHEDULE (PDF)

The committee will make (and adjust) the draws.

Players must be available in the gym 30 minutes prior to the scheduled time to ensure on-time start of matches and also to accommodate schedule adjustments.

Eligibility

Anyone can register, but please note that pretty much all participants will typically belong to the local "B level" category. "Open" players may not find suitable competition at the event.

To qualify for senior events, a player, male or female, must be at least 40 year old by Dec. 31st, 2017.

Rules

Badminton World Federation's laws of badminton.
5 minutes default rule will be strictly enforced; warm-up limited to 3 minutes.
Intervals: 11pt -> 1 minute, between games -> 2 minutes.

The draws for the tournament will be made by the Tournament Committee. The Tournament Committee reserves the right to make the final decision on the draws.

The format of each event will depend on the number of registered entries. Previous editions included ABCD drop flights, AB drop flight, full round-robin, and group round-robin followed by play-offs.

Registration

The registration fees cover the tournament fee ($15) and misc. expenses ($15). Return your payment ($30) and the registration form by March 12th, 2017. You will receive a confirmation by email after registering.

A $5 discount is offered to participants who are less than 19 year old on Dec. 31st 2017, or who are attending college and can show a valid and current college student ID card.

Maximum number of players

To ensure optimal playing conditions, the maximum number of total participants (MS + WS + SMS + SWS) is limited to 48. Moreover, there can not be more than 28 entries per event. First come, first served. Registration will close either on March 12th or when the cap is reached, whichever is earlier.
